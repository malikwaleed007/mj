How it started

Started in Feb 2010, MJ CUSTOMS is the Brain Child of its founder Mr. Jasif Manzoor Sofi who was bored to see all the stock bikes on road and wanted to have a dream bike of his own in a budget. On not finding any good option he decided to jump in the ring and do it himself. Since then MJ CUSTOMS has been delivering dream bikes to customers and bringing smiles to their faces.
